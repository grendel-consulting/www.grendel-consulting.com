Like many other websites, <Business.LegalName/> uses 'cookies'. These cookies are small text files that can be used by websites to make their visitor experience more efficient. They are used to store information including preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Under <abbr title="General Data Protection Regulation">GDPR</abbr> we can store cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for the operation of this site. For all other types of cookies we need, and hence request, your consent.

We use different types of cookies, some of which may be set by services such as PostHog that we use to operate our website. The information collected by our product analytics include <dfn id="ip">internet protocol</dfn> (<abbr>IP</abbr>) addresses, browser type, <dfn id="isp">Internet Service Provider</dfn> (<abbr>ISP</abbr>), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and on-page behaviour.  These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose of the information is for analyzing usage trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information.

You can at any time change or withdraw your consent by contacting us.

Learn more about who we are, how you can contact us and how we process personal data in our <Link to="/privacy">Privacy Policy</Link>.
